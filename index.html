Music and DJing

I started my musician journey as a drummer when I was 15. I took lessons for a year before realizing I loved singing way more than drumming. I joined the musician group of my high school just when I got into it, and it was one of the best experience of my life ! I met people who became a little family year after year. We performed on stage various time and the last concert that I could do with them reunited more than 500 people. Truly an adventure of a lifetime. Now that I'm in college, I sing way less than in high school but occasionaly, you can find me singing in a local bar in Paris. Alongside this activity, I bought myself a DJ controller during the spring of 2022 and after practicing and failing a lot, I finally started DJing at parties ! So if you need a DJ that won't ask you for a huge amount of money but will still play some good tunes, hit me up !
